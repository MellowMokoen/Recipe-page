Title : Chicken & mushroom pasta

Prep time : 10 minutes

Cook time : 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • Pasta- Any kind of your choice
  • 2 chicken breasts -Boneless skinless and diced
  • Sliced mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • Butter - to add a bit of flavour
  • Half chopped onion
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoon flour
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 cup grated cheese
  • A handful amount of parsley
  • Salt and pepper and spices of your choice for seasoning

Instructions

  1. Boil 3 cups of water in a pot, add butter and a teaspoon of salt then pasta. Let it cook for 12 minutes then set aside.
  2. Melt the butter in a pan until hot enough. Season the chicken, add to the pan then brown it.
  3. When the chicken is well browned, remove it and set aside
  4. Add some more butter to the same pan. Add and fry the onion and garlic until soft.
  5. Add the mushrooms and let them cook until they are soft , while you still stir to ensure they do not burn
  6. Now pour the flour and stir. Add milk and keep stirring until the sauce thickens. Add more milk if it becomes too thick.
  7. Add cheese , parsely and salt & pepper to season then stir for the cheese to properly melt
  8. Add the chicken to the sauce then the pasta and mix well
  9. Then serve. And enjoy !
